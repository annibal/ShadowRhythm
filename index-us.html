Shadow Rhythm is a game of Rhythm-Tower Defense style.

The sound Klaus concert, at the same rate, they must perform their rituals through directional to perform their actions. The invocations depend on mana, this one should get them first. When there is enough mana, you can summon monsters. Each has different characteristics and attributes to contribute to strategies against their enemies. Survive on the basis of rhythm + strategy and remove them from the cemetery!



How to Play

  1. Listen to the music
  2. Get Mana's
  3. Do the Ritual (and accumulate units)
  4. Invoke your units (clicking on a lane)
  5. Survive
